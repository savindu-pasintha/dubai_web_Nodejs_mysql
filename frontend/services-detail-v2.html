Service overview

In order to offer our clients the best marine transportation services, our well-trained on-board crew is deployed with the proper health, safety, and environmental protection guidelines.

Our company adheres to environmental laws and ensures that all our vessels are maintained in accordance with the ISM Code. We also provide efficient services at competitive prices to our clients.

Therefore, if you have any specific requirements, please feel free to contact us and we will get back to you in 24 hours with a best quote.

The benefits of multimodal transport are

  • Competitive edge

    Depending on your volumes and requirements, Canava brings you the means of transport that is most competitive for your business

  • Expertise

    A single contact to provide you with precise information on your freight as well as on any unforeseen events

  • Traceability

    Online tracking of your freight in real time.

  • Carbon balance

    A calculation of the carbon footprint is included in every call for tender.
