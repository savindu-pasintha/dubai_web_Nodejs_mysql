Service overview

We offer crew manpower services. Our mission is to provide high-quality crew on a global scale. We supply qualified crew for all ranks and vessels for the shipping industry, for the ship operators, and for the seafarers.

We know very well how important it is to provide the most reliable and responsive service for your needs, as well as to fill every available position at sea and on shore with the right personnel on time.

  • Protection
  • Professionalism

We make sure the crew is well protected by providing proper training, safety equipment and other global standard procedures to ensure the safety of the crew and the vessels.

We at SAMC always make sure that the crew is properly trained and that our personnel would adhere to the standard maritime ethics and principals, ensuring that our clients would experience an efficient and reliable service from the crew.
