What we offer.

Maritime Transportation
We ship all types of roll-on/roll-off cargo such as automobiles, containers on trailers, heavy machinery or equipment on wheels that can be easily rolled on and off through our Ro-Ro vessels for transport.
Cargo Transportation & Handling
Our well-trained on-board crew is deployed with appropriate Health, Safety and Environmental protection guidelines to provide the finest marine transportation services to our customers.
Agency Operations
We ensure that essential supplies, crew transfers, cargo operations, custom documentations and other facilities are arranged with the port authorities systematically.
Manning
We provide the Crew Manning Services. Our mission is to provide high standard quality of crew worldwide.We provide quality crew for all ranks and vessels.
Warehousing
We offer complete marine transportation services. We also own landing crafts which can be used for multipurpose cargo transportation.
