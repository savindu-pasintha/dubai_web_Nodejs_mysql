Service overview

We ship all types of roll-on/roll-off cargo, such as automobiles, containers on trailers, and heavy machinery or equipment on wheels that can be easily rolled on and off through our Ro- Ro vessels for transport.

Roll-on/roll-off vessels transport cargo that can be loaded and unloaded on or near the dock. Roll-on/roll-off vessels use ramps, conveyor belts, or lift gates to transfer cargo. Ro-Ro ships are used for fast and efficient cargo transport. Roll-on/roll-off vessels are often used to transport automobiles, containers on trailers, and heavy machinery or equipment on wheels that can be easily rolled on and off through our Ro-Ro vessels
